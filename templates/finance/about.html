About Our Firm

An independent, fiduciary advisory firm built on trust, transparency, and a relentless commitment to our clients' financial well-being.

Our Story

{{COMPANY_NAME}} was founded in 2008 during one of the most challenging economic periods in modern history. Our founder, Michael A. Harrington, CFA, CFP, left a senior role at a major Wall Street firm with a clear vision: to create an independent registered investment advisory firm that would always put clients first.

What began as a three-person office on Michigan Avenue has grown into a full-service wealth management practice with over $2 billion in assets under management and a team of experienced professionals dedicated to helping more than 500 client families achieve their financial goals.

As an independent RIA, we are free from the conflicts of interest that pervade the brokerage industry. We do not sell proprietary products, earn commissions, or receive compensation from third parties. Our revenue comes solely from transparent, fee-based advisory relationships — ensuring our recommendations are always aligned with your best interests.

Our Fiduciary Commitment

As a registered investment advisor, {{COMPANY_NAME}} is held to a fiduciary standard of care — the highest legal duty in the financial industry. This means we are legally and ethically obligated to act in your best interest at all times, not just recommend "suitable" investments.

We believe this distinction matters. It shapes every aspect of how we work: from our fee-only compensation model that eliminates commissions and conflicts, to our open-architecture investment platform that gives us access to the best solutions available, to our transparent reporting that keeps you fully informed about your portfolio and our performance.
