Local Coverage

Checking, Savings & Everyday Banking

Whether you are opening your first bank account or looking for a better place to grow your savings, our personal banking products are designed with real people in mind — no hidden fees, no fine print surprises, and bankers who actually pick up the phone.

Checking Accounts

  • Free Community Checking — No monthly maintenance fee, no minimum balance. Free debit card, free online and mobile banking, and free bill pay. The account Springfield families have trusted for over 35 years.
  • Interest Checking — Earn a competitive interest rate on balances over $1,500. Includes all the features of Community Checking plus paper statement delivery and cashier's checks at no charge.
  • Student Checking — Designed for ages 16–24. No minimum balance, no monthly fee, and built-in budgeting tools through our mobile app. Parents can link their CRB account for easy transfers.

Savings & Certificates of Deposit

  • Community Savings — Start with as little as $25. Competitive rates with no monthly service charge when you maintain a $100 minimum balance.
  • Money Market Account — Tiered interest rates that reward higher balances. Limited check-writing privileges for easy access to your funds.
  • Certificates of Deposit (CDs) — Terms from 3 months to 60 months. Ask about our CD specials and our no-penalty CD option for added flexibility.
  • IRA Savings & IRA CDs — Traditional and Roth IRA options to help you save for retirement with tax advantages.

Debit Cards & Convenience

  • Visa debit card with chip technology and contactless tap-to-pay
  • Access to 55,000+ surcharge-free ATMs nationwide through the Allpoint network
  • Instant card issue available at all five branch locations
  • Card controls through the CRB mobile app — lock/unlock your card, set spending limits, and receive real-time transaction alerts

Mortgage & Home Loans

Home Financing

Buying a home is one of the biggest decisions you will ever make, and it helps to have a lender who knows the Springfield market inside and out. Our mortgage team lives here, understands local property values, and makes underwriting decisions right here — not in a call center across the country. That means faster approvals and a smoother path to your closing date.

Mortgage Products

  • Conventional Loans — Fixed and adjustable-rate mortgages with competitive rates and flexible terms from 10 to 30 years.
  • FHA Loans — Low down payment options (as low as 3.5%) with more flexible credit requirements. A popular choice for first-time buyers.
  • VA Loans — Zero down payment mortgages for eligible veterans and active-duty service members. No private mortgage insurance required.
  • USDA Loans — Zero down payment financing for homes in eligible rural areas surrounding Springfield, including parts of Christian, Webster, and Your City counties.
  • First-Time Homebuyer Programs — Special rate incentives, down payment assistance coordination, and one-on-one guidance through every step of the process.

Home Equity

  • Home Equity Line of Credit (HELOC) — Tap into your home's equity for renovations, education expenses, or debt consolidation with a flexible revolving line.
  • Home Equity Loan — A fixed-rate, lump-sum loan secured by your home equity. Predictable monthly payments for planned expenses.

Construction Loans

  • One-time close construction-to-permanent loans
  • Lot loans for land purchase prior to construction
  • Builder financing for local contractors and developers
Agricultural Lending

Agricultural Banking

The Ozarks region has a proud agricultural heritage, and {{COMPANY_NAME}} has been supporting area farmers and ranchers since our founding. Our agricultural lending team understands the unique cycles, risks, and opportunities of farming — because many of them grew up on farms themselves.

  • Farm Operating Loans — Annual and multi-year operating lines to cover seed, feed, fertilizer, fuel, and other input costs. Structured around your production cycle.
  • Farm Real Estate Loans — Financing for land purchases, including pastureland, cropland, and timberland. Competitive rates with terms up to 25 years.
  • Equipment Financing — Loans and leases for tractors, combines, irrigation systems, livestock handling equipment, and other farm machinery.
  • Livestock Loans — Financing for herd expansion, feeder cattle purchases, and breeding stock acquisition.
  • FSA Guaranteed Loans — CRB works with the USDA Farm Service Agency to provide guaranteed loans for farmers who may not qualify for conventional agricultural credit.
  • Crop Insurance Coordination — We work with your crop insurance agent to ensure your coverage aligns with your lending structure and risk management plan.
Digital Banking

Online & Mobile Banking

Community banking and modern technology are not mutually exclusive. Our digital banking platform gives you 24/7 access to your accounts from anywhere, while our branches and local bankers are always just a phone call or a short drive away when you need them.

  • Mobile Banking App — Available for iOS and Android. Check balances, view transactions, transfer funds, and deposit checks with your phone's camera.
  • Online Banking — Full-featured account management from any web browser. View statements, download transaction history, and manage account alerts.
  • Online Bill Pay — Schedule one-time or recurring payments to any payee. Eliminate the hassle of writing and mailing checks.
  • Mobile Deposit — Deposit checks anytime by snapping a photo with the CRB mobile app. Funds are typically available the next business day.
  • Zelle — Send and receive money directly from your CRB checking account using just an email address or phone number. Fast, safe, and free.
  • Account Alerts — Customizable email and push notifications for low balances, large transactions, direct deposits, and more.
  • eStatements — Paperless statements delivered securely to your online banking profile. Accessible anytime for up to seven years.
