Investment Advisory

Wealth Management

Our wealth management service is the foundation of every client relationship. We begin with a deep understanding of your complete financial picture — assets, liabilities, income, expenses, risk tolerance, and goals — and construct a personalized investment strategy designed to grow and protect your wealth over time.

We employ a disciplined, evidence-based investment approach that combines strategic asset allocation with tactical adjustments informed by market conditions and economic outlook. Our open-architecture platform provides access to institutional-quality investments across all asset classes, free from proprietary product constraints.

  • Customized portfolio construction and ongoing management
  • Strategic asset allocation and periodic rebalancing
  • Risk assessment and tolerance profiling
  • Alternative investment evaluation and selection
  • Concentrated stock position management
  • Performance monitoring and quarterly reporting
Comprehensive Planning

Financial Planning

A comprehensive financial plan is the framework that connects your investment strategy, tax planning, insurance coverage, and estate documents into a cohesive whole. Our planning process begins with an in-depth analysis of where you are today and builds a clear roadmap toward your long-term goals.

We revisit and adjust your plan regularly as your circumstances evolve, ensuring that every financial decision you make is informed by the broader context of your life and objectives. Our Director of Financial Planning, Sarah Chen, CFP, leads this process for every client engagement.

  • Comprehensive financial plan development and annual review
  • Cash flow analysis and budgeting framework
  • Education funding strategies (529 plans, UGMA/UTMA)
  • Insurance review and risk management assessment
  • Net worth tracking and milestone planning
  • Coordination with your CPA, attorney, and other advisors
Retirement Strategy

Retirement Planning

Whether you are decades away from retirement or approaching it in the near term, our retirement planning services are designed to give you clarity and confidence about your financial future. We develop detailed retirement income plans that address the complex interplay between Social Security timing, pension benefits, investment withdrawals, and tax efficiency.

Our planning process uses sophisticated modeling tools to stress-test your retirement plan against a wide range of scenarios, including market downturns, inflation spikes, extended longevity, and healthcare cost increases.

  • 401(k), IRA, and employer plan optimization
  • Social Security timing and claiming strategies
  • Retirement income projection and gap analysis
  • Roth conversion analysis and multi-year planning
  • Withdrawal sequencing and tax-bracket management
  • Healthcare and long-term care cost planning
Tax Optimization

Tax Strategy

Taxes are often the single largest drag on investment returns, yet many investors fail to incorporate tax strategy into their financial planning. At {{COMPANY_NAME}}, tax optimization is not an afterthought — it is woven into every investment decision and planning recommendation we make.

Our Director of Tax Strategy, Michael Rivera, CPA, works closely with your CPA and estate attorney to coordinate a holistic approach to minimizing your lifetime tax burden. From tax-loss harvesting and asset location strategies to Roth conversions and charitable giving, we identify and execute opportunities to keep more of what you earn.

  • Tax-loss harvesting and gain deferral strategies
  • Charitable giving strategies (donor-advised funds, QCDs, CRTs)
  • Trust structures for tax efficiency
  • Business owner tax planning and entity structuring
  • Roth conversion analysis and multi-year projections
  • Coordination with your CPA and tax professionals
Legacy & Transfer

Estate Planning

Estate planning is about far more than minimizing taxes — it is about ensuring that your values, wishes, and legacy are carried forward in the way you intend. Our Director of Estate Planning, Jennifer Walsh, JD, works with you and your estate attorney to develop a coordinated plan that addresses wealth transfer, asset protection, incapacity planning, and philanthropic goals.

For families with significant wealth, the complexity of estate planning increases substantially. Issues such as generation-skipping transfer taxes, irrevocable trust structures, family limited partnerships, and private foundation management require specialized knowledge and careful coordination among your advisory team.

  • Wealth transfer and gifting strategies
  • Trust administration and coordination
  • Legacy planning and philanthropic structuring
  • Family governance and multi-generational planning
  • Business succession planning
  • Beneficiary designation review and optimization
