Our Story

{{COMPANY_NAME}} was founded in 2008, in the midst of the worst financial crisis since the Great Depression. While many investors watched their portfolios collapse and their advisors scramble, our founding partners saw a glaring need: transparent, client-first advisory services free from the conflicts of interest that had eroded public trust in the financial industry.

David Thornton and Sarah Chen left established positions at major financial institutions to build a firm grounded in a simple principle — act as a fiduciary, charge transparent fees, and never accept commissions. What began as a two-person practice has grown into a full-service wealth management firm with over $850 million in assets under management, serving more than 400 client families across the United States.

As a fee-only registered investment advisor, {{COMPANY_NAME}} does not sell proprietary products or earn commissions from third parties. Our revenue comes exclusively from transparent advisory fees — ensuring that every recommendation we make is driven solely by what is best for you.

Our Fiduciary Commitment

As a registered investment advisor, {{COMPANY_NAME}} is held to a fiduciary standard of care — the highest legal duty in the financial industry. This means we are legally and ethically obligated to act in your best interest at all times, not simply recommend "suitable" investments.

We believe this distinction matters. It shapes every aspect of how we operate: our fee-only compensation model eliminates commissions and conflicts, our open-architecture investment platform gives us access to the best solutions available regardless of provider, and our transparent reporting keeps you fully informed about your portfolio and our performance at every step.
